03.25

Built my own digital marketing agency from scratch with $0 budget. Created a complete business system including website design, payment processing, email marketing, and social media strategy to sell digital marketing templates and services to Moldovan businesses.

Project Goals:

Project 04
Redesigning the home essentials shopping experience - where comprehensive research meets user-centered design innovation.

scroll horizontally to explore the project

Casamare

Brief - Casamare

06.22 - 09.22

Redesigned a comprehensive home essentials e-commerce platform offering everything from kitchen appliances to home décor. Conducted thorough competitor analysis and user research to create a unique, memorable shopping experience that stands out in a crowded market.

Project Goals:

Create a modern, memorable platform that differentiates from cookie-cutter e-commerce designs.

Build intuitive user flows for a vast product catalog spanning multiple home categories.

Design effective information architecture that makes product discovery effortless.

Design decisions

Broke away from standard e-commerce layouts after discovering most competitors used identical structures. Developed a unique information architecture that groups related items into comprehensive categories, making navigation intuitive despite the extensive product range.

Visit:

sipunct.com
Key takeaways:

Research reveals opportunities: Comprehensive competitor analysis showed that differentiation through unique layout and structure could create significant competitive advantage.

Information architecture is everything: With thousands of products across multiple categories, organizing content logically became more important than visual design elements.

User testing validates assumptions: Conducting usability tests confirmed that the non-standard approach actually improved user experience rather than confusing customers.

My Design Process

I believe great projects start with great communication. That's why I've developed a clear, collaborative process that keeps you informed and engaged every step of the way—from understanding your vision to delivering results that exceed expectations.

scroll down

step #1

duration: 1-2 days

Inquiry & Discovery

Fill out the brief form down below with details about your project—whether you need a brand-new website, a visual identity refresh, or specific design collateral.

We’ll schedule a short call to talk about your business goals, target audience, and any design preferences or inspirations.

step #2

duration: 1-2 days

Proposal & Onboarding

After our call, I’ll craft a proposal that outlines design deliverables (like wireframes, mood boards, or mockups), timelines, and pricing options.

Once approved, we’ll set up a dedicated communication channel (e.g., WhatsApp/Telegram) and collect any assets (logo files, brand guidelines, content) you already have.

step #3

duration: 4-8 days

Strategy & Design

Using your brand goals and preferences, I’ll develop initial design concepts—this could be website wireframes, UI mockups, or graphic layouts.

We’ll refine the designs through feedback rounds to ensure everything aligns with your brand identity, user experience needs, and marketing goals.

step #4

duration: 2-3 days

Hand-off

You’ll receive all finalized files—website launch support, brand guidelines, and any additional graphics or templates.

The outcome?

A professional online presence or cohesive visual identity, plus the peace of mind that you have ongoing support if you need it.
